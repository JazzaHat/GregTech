Dragon Egg Energy Siphon

"Harness the teleportation Power of the Dragon Egg!"
Generates EU by harnessing the Incredible Teleportation Power of the Dragon Egg.
Fuel Type
 EU output
Dragon Egg
 1024 EU/t, infinitely

The Dragon Egg must be placed directly on top of the Siphon.
Outputs energy at a rate of 1024 EU/t.
Accepts Covers.

Usage Tips

Harnessing the Incredible Teleportation Power of the Dragon Egg angers the local environment. If Thaumcraft 3 is installed, when active, the Dragon Egg Energy Siphon will generate Flux at an extreme rate, rapidly tainting nearby Aura Nodes.
Note: As of yet, this does not occur in Thaumcraft 4.

Only one Dragon Egg Energy Siphon can be active in a world. There is an anti-cheat protection, making a second Siphon explode upon activation.
This anti-cheat protection can be disabled in Dynamic Config.cfg (as you may have more than one legitimately acquired Egg if you have MystCraft), with the line
B:DragonEggEnergySiphon.AllowMultipleEggs_false=true
