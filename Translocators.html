Electric Translocator:

Moves items from inventory adjacent to the green side to the inventory adjacent to the red side.
Accepts covers and upgrades.

Its GUI allows item filtering, so you can make it move only certain items, up to 9.
Clicking the " !-|->" icon makes the filter to be inverted, causing the translocator to move everything but the items on the filter.
Clicking the blue energy button makes the translocator to output/not output energy to the colored sides.

Maximum EU input : 32 EU/p , Transformer upgrades quadruples this value.
Maximum EU output : 32 EU/t, Transformer upgrades quadruples this value. First transformer upgrade will make it output 4 packets instead of quadrupling the packet size

Recipe :


Required Items
 4x Electrum plates
 2x Electronic Circuit
 1x RE-Battery
 1x Conveyor Module
 1x Machine Block

Advanced Translocator :

Pretty much as its predecessor, it moves items from green to red side with filtering.
The difference is that you can choose from which side of the adjacent inventory you want to pull/insert items.
For example if you choose Top on the red side, you will insert items like the translocator were on Top, even if it is not. Same applies for the green side.
Accepts covers and upgrades.
Clicking the " !-|->" icon makes the filter to be inverted, causing the translocator to move everything but the items on the filter.
Clicking the blue energy button makes the translocator to output/not output energy to the colored sides.

Maximum EU input : 32 EU/p , Transformer upgrades quadruples this value.
Maximum EU output : 32 EU/t, Transformer upgrades quadruples this value. First transformer upgrade will make it output 4 packets instead of quadrupling the packet size

Recipe:


Required Items
 4x Electrum Plates
 2x Advanced Circuit
 1x RE-Battery
 1x Conveyor Module
 1x Advanced Machine block
Alternative Items
2x Advanced Circuit (UE)
 1x Battery (UE)
1x Titanium/Steel Machine Hull
