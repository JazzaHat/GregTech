Note: Thaumcraft 4 fuels are possibly bugged.
  • Mercury and Liquid XP can be piped in.
  • Output at a rate of 24 EU/t.
  • Accepts liquids from Liquiducts and Waterproof Pipes.
  • Accepts Covers.

Block usage tip : If you have either TC4 or Ars Magica, try to get one as soon as you can.
If you have a mob farm, Liquid XP can be a good way to generate energy.
