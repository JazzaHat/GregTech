Battery Chargers are a series of machines which recharge EU-using devices and both recharge and discharge batteries. They are each iterations of the base Charger Box, with storage capacity increasing with each subsequent model. All chargers emit 32 EU/t and can receive the same unless upgraded. Due to their variable storage capacity, chargers make effective alternatives to MFE's and MFSU's.
