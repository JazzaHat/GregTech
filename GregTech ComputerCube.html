The GregTech ComputerCube runs G.L.A.D-OS (Graphical Lag Auto-detecting OS). It has several functions that are explained in the main page of the ComputerCube.
Press the M button to swap between modes :
  • First mode, the starting GUI, which does absolutely nothing.
  • Second mode, the reactorplanner, which simulates nuclear reactors, 99.99% failsafe, requires a bit of energy to simulate.
  • Third mode, the Seedbag Scanner, which scans provided seed bags.
  • Fourth/Fifth mode, the in-game recipe list for Centrifuge/Fusion reactor respectively.
  • Sixth mode, the in-game "wiki" with basic explanations of some blocks.
  • Seventh mode, the in-game recipe list for Electrolyzer.
From the Third to Seventh mode, the screen will have constantly moving "texts", the more it flickers/moves, the laggier the chunk it is placed is, graphically.
