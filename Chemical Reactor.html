The Chemical Reactor combines two chemicals, sometimes fuctioning as an opposite to the Industrial Electrolyzer.

Notable uses of the Chemical Reactor are to create chemicals used for processing other materials or to create fuels. It can also be used to produce IC2 Fertilizer from various chemicals and dusts.
