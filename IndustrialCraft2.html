IndustrialCraft2 is Minecraft mod. Developed by Alblaka and his IC2 team, IndustrialCraft2 is the parent mod of GregTech-Addon. It is well known as one of the major tech mods around, and is particularly popular in modpacks. Major features include but are not limited to: various tiered machinery, the E-Net, electric tools and armor, nuclear reactors, and revolutionary agricultural development.
