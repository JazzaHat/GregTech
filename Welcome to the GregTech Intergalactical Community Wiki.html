IMPORTANT NOTICE TO THOSE PLAYING GREGTECH 5 AND ABOVE!



Welcome to the Gregtech Community Wiki. GregTech is an addon, written by GregoriusT, to the IndustrialCraft2 mod of the game Minecraft. Things to note:

  • This wiki is community-run, and eternally a work in progress.
  • Sadly, due to past vandalism, this is a protected wiki, and as such you must be a member in order to edit pages. To apply, sign up in the top left.
  • If you want to help (which would be very appreciated), just apply for membership and an organizer will accept you as soon as possible.
  • This wiki will contain information pertaining to GregTech 4.08s and below, for Minecraft 1.6.X. All Information pertaining to GregTech 5.00 and above, for Minecraft 1.7.2, will reside on the Official FTB Wiki.

Download and Thread

  • In order to download the mod (currently compatible with Minecraft 1.6.X, and with open alpha versions for Minecraft 1.7.2 and the experimental builds of IndustrialCraft2), we recommend that you visit the GregTech thread of the IndustrialCraft2 forums. (And while you're on the thread, please read Greg's first two posts, in particular, the FAQ.)
