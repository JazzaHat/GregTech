"Harvests the Cropsticks and Logs in front of it"

The Crop Harvestor is a machine for automatic harvesting of your Crops, in particular, IC2 Cropsticks. When the plant in front of it reaches maximum growth, the Crop Harvestor will automatically harvest it (as a right-click would). The produced items are only picked up by the Harvestor if they fall in the block it is facing.
Items are stored internally and can be ejected through the Red output side, or removed by Conveyor Modules or Pipes.

Cost per Crop harvested: 100 EU

Overclocker

  • Accepts Covers

The range of the Harvestor can be increased by installing Overclocker Upgrades. Each Overclocker Upgrade doubles its range (1x OC means 2 tiles, 2x OC = 4 tiles, 3x OC = 8 tiles, 4x OC = 16 tiles).
Up to 4 Overclocker Upgrades can be installed.
Unlike in conventional machines, each Overclocker Upgrade increases the energy required for each operation by x4. This means that at 4 Overclocker Upgrades, each crop harvested consumes 25,600EU, and would require Battery Upgrades to even be carried out.

Via its GUI, it can be told whether or not to automatically output items and EU (through the Red side), or whether to emit a Redstone Signal when its inventory is occupied.
