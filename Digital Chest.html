Digital Chest

The Digital Chest can only accept any one item, but a large amount of it. The default is 32 768 (8x of a Factorization Barrel), but it is configurable.

Properties

  • Accepts automation of any kind. Has internal filter to prevent wrong items being inserted.
  • Accepts covers.
  • Does not require energy.
  • Right-click the block to check how many items are stored.
  • Left click to pull out 1 stack.
  • Right click with item you want to put in to put stack in.
  • Double right click to put all of that item in your inventory in.

Recipe


  • 7 x Iron Plates
  • 1 x Data Orb
  • 1 x Computer Monitor
  • Produces 1 x Digital Chest

  • 7 x Aluminium Plates
  • 1 x Data Orb
  • 1 x Computer Monitor
  • Produces 1 x Digital Chest
