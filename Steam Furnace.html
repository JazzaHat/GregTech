"Smelting things with compressed Steam"
Steam Capacity: 16 Buckets
Steam may be piped in any direction except the front.
Like most steam machines, the exhaust vent must face a block of air for the machine to perform more than one operation. The exhaust vent may be repositioned with a wrench. Upon completing an operation, a dangerously hot puff of smoke will come out of the vent. This will deal 6hp (3 hearts).
Every tick it uses 4 Liters of steam.
Operations last 12.8 seconds.
