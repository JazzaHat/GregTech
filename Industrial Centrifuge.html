The Industrial Centrifuge is a machine that spins resources at high speeds, separating them into their more basic components. Based on its input, the Industrial Centrifuge produces up to four new items over a certain period of time requiring power for each operation.

Many products of the Industrial Centrifuge can be further processed in the Industrial Electrolyzer.

Industrial Centrifuges are one of the primary components in the infrastructure required to make and support a Fusion Reactor, capable of producing the Deuterium, Tritium, and Helium-3 used as fuels.

Industrial Centrifuges can also be used to extract Rubber from Sticky Resin or Rubber Wood, much more efficiently than a standard Extractor.

Maximum Input: 32 EU/t (LV)
This machine consumes 5 EU/t for all recipes, allowing up to 6 Industrial Centrifuges to be safely supported and run simultaneously on one 32 EU/t line.

Recipe:


4 Stainless Steel Plates
2 Advanced Circuits
2 Steel/Stainless Steel Hulls
1 Extractor (Can be Automatic or IC2)

Crafting Recipe Note:
Crafting an Industrial Centrifuge requires Chrome and Manganese Dusts to make Stainless Steel, however the paths used to acquire both of these dusts include an Industrial Centrifuge. In order to obtain the Manganese Dust, either hunt through dungeon chests, or hunt Black Granite or the End for Tungstate Ore, and feed that through either a Universal Macerator or a Sturdy Grinder, to obtain the necessary Manganese Dust. In order to obtain the Chrome Dust, either hunt through dungeon chests or craft an Industrial Electrolyzer and electrolyze Ruby Dust.

Methane Production

One of the main uses of the Industrial Centrifuge is to make methane from food/organic produce. Raw food is better at making methane then cooked!
Although these recipes are time-consuming, they do provide more energy in Methane Cells than is used to make them (provided the food can be acquired freely).
