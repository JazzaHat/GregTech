Digital Tank

The Digital Tank can only hold one type of liquid, but a large amount of it. The default is 256000 Millibuckets (or 256 Buckets), but it is configurable.

Properties

  • Accepts liquids from any liquid transferring device, such as liquiducts or pipes.
  • Has internal filter to prevent different liquids being inserted.
  • Accepts covers.
  • Does not require energy.
  • Right-click the block to open the GUI and check how many millibuckets are stored
  • You can fill liquid containers by placing them on top slot if there is a valid filled container.

Recipe

The recipe used to craft a Digital Chest but was moved to craft the Digital Tank when it was added as the surprise block in Gregtech version 4.05o.
Currently the Quantum Tank still uses a Quantum Chest to be crafted, but that may now change (maybe).

7 x Steel Plates or Stainless Steel Plates
1 x Data Orb
1 x Computer Monitor
