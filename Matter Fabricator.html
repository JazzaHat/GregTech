Removed as of IC² Experimental UUM rework.

UU Matter in IC2 Experimental is a liquid now, and costs 10,000,000 EU per bucket (which was the cost of UUM under the Matter Fabricator anyway). This page is currently kept as a reference to those still on Minecraft 1.5.2

The Matter Fabricator is the GregTech alternative to the IndustrialCraft2 Mass Fabricator
IC² Mass Fabricator is disabled by default in favor of GregTech one, this can be reversed on the config if you want easy mode.
The Matter Fabricator, like the Mass Fabricator, makes UU-Matter except with a couple twists:

  1. The Matter Fabricator requires an amplifier (such as Scrap) and the amplifier doesn't decrease the amount of EU consumed per UUM
  2. The Matter Fabricator by default costs 16,666,666 EU/UU-Matter (vs. the 1,000,000 of the Mass Fabricator, or the 166,667 if you had scrap)
  3. It has a maximum EU/t (and packet size) of 8192 EU.
