As a contributor to this wiki, you agree to abide by these rules:

  1. Do not post outdated information. This especially applies to copying information over from other wikis. please verify that your information is consistent with the most recent version before editing
  2. When making major changes to a page's format, post on the discussion page. Just a simple explanation of what you are doing, and why you think it is better than the current format will suffice.
  3. Keep your pictures small. We have a limited storage space on this wiki, and though we are not currently in any danger of running out, this could be a concern in the future. Furthermore, we want to be accommodating to people with slower computers and smaller monitors. Bear in mind when taking pictures, that not everyone has a high-resolution computer. As a general rule, about 12 cm or 5 inches on a side should be the maximum, preferably less, though exceptions can be made for particularly important pictures.
